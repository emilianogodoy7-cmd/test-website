HOW IT WORKS

From choosing your panels to having them installed — here's exactly what to expect at every step.

The Process

Simple from Start to Finish

Most projects go from first contact to delivery in under two weeks. Here's the full picture so you know exactly what's involved.

1

Pick a Style

Browse our full panel catalogue and find the finish that fits your space. Not sure where to start? Tell us the room, the vibe you're going for, and your budget — we'll narrow it down for you.

  • Visit our panels page to see every collection side-by-side
  • Order a free physical sample before committing — see and feel it in your actual lighting
  • Mix finishes across rooms or use one consistent look throughout
Pro Tip

Hold a sample against your wall at different times of day. Warm-toned panels can shift dramatically under artificial evening light compared to morning sun.

Your Time Investment

15–30 minutes browsing. Samples arrive within 3–5 business days if requested.

2

Send Measurements

Accurate measurements are the most important part of the process. They determine how many panels you need and ensure nothing is wasted. Here's how to measure correctly.

  • Measure the total width of each wall in metres or feet — floor to ceiling, wall to wall
  • Note door and window positions so we can account for cut-outs and trim pieces
  • A rough sketch or phone photo of the space is more than enough — no architect drawings needed
  • If covering a full room, measure each wall separately and label them (e.g. North Wall, Feature Wall)
Measuring Tip

Always measure twice. We recommend adding 10% to your total square footage as a buffer for cuts, waste, and future repairs.

Your Time Investment

10–20 minutes with a tape measure. Submit via the quote form or email us a photo.

3

Get Your Quote

Within 48 business hours of receiving your measurements, we'll send a detailed, itemised quote. No vague estimates — you'll see exactly what you're paying for before committing to anything.

  • Panel quantity and cost broken down by wall or room
  • Accessories included (corner trims, end profiles, joining strips) with pricing
  • Delivery estimate and any applicable shipping fees
  • Optional installation add-on if you'd prefer a professional finish
What to Ask Us

Ask about bulk pricing if you're covering multiple rooms — orders above a certain m² threshold typically qualify for a volume discount.

Turnaround Time

Quote delivered within 48 business hours. Complex multi-room projects may take up to 72 hours.

4

Confirm & Order

Happy with the quote? Give us the go-ahead and we'll begin preparing your order. Panels are cut and packed to order — this isn't off-the-shelf stock, so quality is consistent every time.

  • A deposit is required to begin production — balance due before dispatch
  • We confirm your order in writing with a full itemised receipt
  • Any last-minute changes to quantities or finishes can be made within 24 hours of order placement
Order Tip

Double-check your colour choice one final time before confirming. Custom-cut panels cannot be returned once production begins.

Production Time

Standard orders: 3–5 business days. Large or custom orders: 5–7 business days.

5

Delivery & Install

Panels arrive carefully packed and labelled, ready to mount. Most homeowners install DIY in a single day. If you'd prefer a professional result without the effort, our installation team can handle everything.

  • Panels ship with installation instructions and a recommended adhesive/screw spec sheet
  • DIY installation requires basic tools: level, tape measure, construction adhesive or drill
  • Panels are lightweight — a standard 9.5 ft panel weighs under 2 kg
  • Professional installers available in Montreal and surrounding areas
Install Tip

Start from the most visible corner and work outward. Use a level on every third panel — small errors compound over a large wall.

Delivery + Install Timeline

Delivery: 2–4 business days after dispatch. DIY install of a standard room: 4–8 hours.
