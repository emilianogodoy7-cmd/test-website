Description

These decorative PVC beams replicate the warm, natural appearance of solid wood while offering far superior practical properties. Lightweight and easy to handle, they can be installed by a single person on ceilings, in entranceways, or as freestanding room dividers in open-plan spaces.

Unlike natural wood, PVC beams resist moisture, fire, and insects — making them an ideal low-maintenance alternative. Available in Nogal Americano (American walnut) and Aregue finishes.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: PVC
  • Standard size: 9.84 Ft × 1.91 Inch × 3.94 Inch
  • Finishes: Nogal Americano, Aregue
  • Moisture resistant: Yes — suitable for humid environments
  • Fire resistant: Yes
  • Insect resistant: Yes
  • Applications: Ceilings, entryways, living rooms, commercial spaces, room dividers
