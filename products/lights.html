Description

Lighting is the finishing touch that elevates a wall panel installation from functional to truly impressive. Our LED light profiles are designed to integrate with wall panel systems, highlighting textures, casting warm shadow plays across fluted surfaces, and adding depth to any room.

The exterior wall panel light extends this ambiance to outdoor facades and patios, allowing you to showcase your exterior cladding even after dark.

Features & Specifications

  • Technology: LED — energy-efficient and long-lasting
  • LED Light Profile (LLP-01): Integrated profile for indoor wall panel systems
  • Exterior Wall Panel Light (EWPL-002): Outdoor LED for exterior facades and patios
  • Compatibility: Designed for use with all SuperDeco Canada wall panel systems
