Description

Our acoustic panels combine real wood slats over a dense recycled-PET felt backing — a proven combination that softens echo, improves voice clarity, and adds visual warmth to any room.

Perfect for home offices that double as video-call studios, podcast and music recording spaces, conference rooms, and media rooms. The slatted wood face looks sharp on its own and disappears acoustically into the background, doing its job without drawing attention to itself.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: MDF wood slats on recycled-PET felt backing
  • Standard size: 2400 mm × 600 mm × 22 mm
  • Sound absorption: NRC 0.85 — reduces echo by up to 70%
  • Installation: Screw-mount or construction adhesive
  • Eco-credentials: Felt made from recycled plastic bottles
  • Maintenance: Vacuum with brush attachment as needed
  • Warranty: 10-year manufacturer warranty
