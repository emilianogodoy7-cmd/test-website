Description

Designed to withstand Canadian weather year-round, our outdoor panels are made from wood-plastic composite (WPC) — a tough, low-maintenance material that resists rain, snow, and UV without warping, cracking, or fading.

Use them for full-house facades, garden walls, balconies, or patio feature walls. They install over standard battens and arrive ready to mount. The clean horizontal-line profile lends a modern, architectural look to any exterior.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: Wood-Plastic Composite (WPC) with UV-protective coating
  • Standard size: 3000 mm × 156 mm × 21 mm (custom cuts available)
  • Weather rating: Suitable for -40°C to +60°C
  • Installation: Mounted on horizontal battens with stainless screws
  • Water absorption: < 0.5% — no swelling or rot
  • Maintenance: Rinse with garden hose — no sealing or staining required
  • Warranty: 15-year manufacturer warranty
