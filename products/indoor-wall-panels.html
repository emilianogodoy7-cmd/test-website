Description

Our indoor wall panels combine the warmth of natural wood with the practicality of modern PVC. Precision-milled fluted profiles cast soft, dynamic shadows across your wall — adding texture, dimension, and quiet sophistication to any interior.

Available in over a dozen finishes from light oak to deep walnut, panels arrive ready to mount and can typically be installed by a single person in a few hours. The surface is moisture-resistant, easy to wipe clean, and built to retain its appearance for years.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: High-density PVC with wood-grain laminate finish
  • Standard size: 2900 mm × 122 mm × 12 mm (custom cuts available)
  • Coverage: Approx. 0.35 m² per panel
  • Installation: Adhesive mount or screw fixing — both supported
  • Fire rating: Class B s2 d0 (low flame spread)
  • Maintenance: Wipe clean with damp cloth — no special products required
  • Warranty: 10-year manufacturer warranty
