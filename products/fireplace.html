Electric Fireplace

Wall-mounted or recessed electric fireplace with a stunning 3D multicolor LED flame effect — bringing warmth and ambiance to Canadian living rooms and bedrooms without requiring a chimney.

1500W 120V Canadian Compatible Remote Control

Description

This sleek electric fireplace offers all the warmth and visual drama of a traditional fireplace — without the installation complexity, chimney requirements, or ongoing fuel costs. The 3D multicolor LED flame effect is remarkably realistic and can be adjusted or operated independently of the heat function.

Designed for Canadian 120V electrical systems, the unit can be wall-mounted flush or recessed into a feature wall for a seamless, built-in look. Overheat protection and a programmable 8-hour timer make it safe and convenient for everyday use.

Features & Specifications

  • Power: 1500W
  • Voltage: 120V (Canadian compatible)
  • Dimensions: 60" × 5.9" × 17.7" (152 × 15 × 45 cm)
  • Flame effect: 3D multicolor LED — adjustable colour and intensity
  • Installation: Wall-mounted or recessed
  • Heating area: 160–270 ft² (15–25 m²)
  • Controls: Remote control & touch panel
  • Heat modes: Flame only / Low / High
  • Timer: Programmable up to 8 hours
  • Safety: Overheat protection built in
  • Front material: Tempered glass + metal frame
