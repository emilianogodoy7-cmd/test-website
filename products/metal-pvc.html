Metal PVC

Metallic-finish PVC panels that add an industrial-chic or brushed-metal aesthetic. Perfect for accent walls and commercial spaces.

Brushed Finish Lightweight Multiple Tones
Request Quote Order Free Sample

Description

The look and feel of brushed metal — at PVC weight and price. Our metal-finish panels capture the directional grain and warm sheen of real aluminum, copper, and bronze surfaces, with a tactile texture that holds up under close inspection.

Ideal for restaurant feature walls, retail backdrops, modern lofts, kitchen splashbacks, and any space where you want an industrial edge without the cost or installation complexity of true sheet metal.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: PVC core with brushed metallic laminate finish
  • Standard size: 2440 mm × 1220 mm × 3 mm
  • Finishes: Brushed Aluminum, Antique Copper, Bronze, Gunmetal
  • Weight: ~1.8 kg per sheet — easy single-person handling
  • Installation: Adhesive mount; cuts cleanly with utility knife
  • Maintenance: Soft cloth; non-abrasive cleaner only
  • Warranty: 7-year manufacturer warranty
