Description

WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) fence panels combine the aesthetic warmth of natural wood with the durability of modern composite materials. These fences are designed for installation on cement surfaces using a metal base with screw-mounted support for a secure, permanent attachment.

Suitable for terraces, patios, commercial areas, and urban gardens, WPC fences are highly weather resistant — able to withstand Canadian winters, UV exposure, and moisture without warping, cracking, or requiring regular treatment.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: WPC (Wood Plastic Composite)
  • Installation: Metal base with screw-mounted support for cement surfaces
  • Weather resistance: High — UV-resistant and water-resistant
  • Applications: Terraces, patios, commercial areas, urban gardens
  • Style compatibility: Contemporary and classic designs
  • Maintenance: No painting, staining, or regular treatment required
