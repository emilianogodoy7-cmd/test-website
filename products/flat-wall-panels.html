Flat Wall Panels

Smooth flat-profile PVC panels with rich wood-look and metallic finishes — a clean, minimal aesthetic for contemporary Canadian interiors.

Description

Flat wall panels offer all the warmth and character of decorative wall cladding without the fluted profile. The smooth surface reflects light evenly, making spaces feel open and modern — ideal for minimalist, Scandinavian, and contemporary interiors.

Available in nine finishes including classic wood tones, metallic accents, and pure black, these panels are simple to install and easy to maintain. The wide 15.74-inch profile means fewer seams and faster coverage.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: High-density PVC with laminate finish
  • Standard size: 15.74 Inch × 9.5 Ft (custom cuts available)
  • Profile: Flat (smooth surface, no fluting)
  • Installation: Adhesive mount or clip-on system
  • Finishes: 9 options — wood tones, metallic, and solid colours
  • Maintenance: Wipe clean with damp cloth
  • Warranty: 10-year manufacturer warranty
