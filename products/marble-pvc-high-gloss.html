Marble PVC High Gloss

Premium Finish 20+ Patterns Easy Install
Request Quote Order Free Sample

Description

Get the look of Carrara, Calacatta, or Emperador marble — without the cost, weight, or sealing maintenance of real stone. Our high-gloss marble PVC panels are printed and laminated using a process that captures the depth and movement of natural veining with stunning realism.

Use them to elevate hotel lobbies, restaurant interiors, primary bathrooms, kitchen backsplashes, or any space that calls for a luxury statement. Panels arrive ready to install with concealed seam joints, so the finished wall reads as a continuous slab.

Features & Specifications

  • Material: High-density PVC core with UV-printed marble laminate
  • Standard size: 2440 mm × 1220 mm × 4 mm
  • Patterns: 20+ marble looks including Carrara, Calacatta, Emperador
  • Finish: High-gloss with anti-scratch protective coating
  • Installation: Construction adhesive on flat substrate
  • Maintenance: Wipe with damp cloth — no sealing required
  • Warranty: 10-year manufacturer warranty
