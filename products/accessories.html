Description

The right accessories are what separate a good installation from a great one. Our comprehensive range of finishing profiles covers every transition, corner, and edge scenario — from clean interior corners to exposed panel ends, joining seams, and round-profile edges.

All profiles are available in three finishes — black, gold, and aluminium — so you can match or complement any panel from our collection. Installation clips ensure consistent panel spacing and a secure, long-lasting mount.

Available Profiles

  • End Lines (LTI/LTD): Left and right edge finishing trims
  • T-Wall Corners: Interior and exterior corner covers
  • U-Corner Profiles: Snap-on U-shaped corner finish (8 Ft × 3/16")
  • L-Corner Profiles: Angle corner trim for neat edge transitions
  • Union Profiles: Panel joining strips for seamless seam coverage
  • Round Corner Profiles: Smooth, radiused edge finish
  • Installation Clips (A-01): Secure clip-on mounting system
  • Finishes: Black, Gold, Silver / Aluminium
